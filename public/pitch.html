02 / The Insight

If we can factory-build software,
we can factory-build the sale.

The “SDLC factory” automates the grunt work of producing software so engineers stay on the design. Sales Factory does the same for sales ops.

The rep stays human on the call. The factory does the rest.
03 / How Sales Factory Solves It

One call in. Every deliverable out.

JOIN

Recall.ai bot

Auto-joins the call and streams the live transcript to the relay.

COACH

Live nudges

The coordinator agent posts coaching to Slack on objections, pricing, competitors & buying signals.

BUILD

The swarm

quote · deck · order · research build real Salesforce Quotes, a clickable deck & an order form.

VERIFY

Self-grade

It grades its own work against a rubric until satisfied — rerunnable on any transcript.
